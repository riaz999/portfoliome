Busy

                but not in the way
                most people accept.
                I’m busy calming my fear
                and finding my courage.
                I’m busy listening to my kids.
                I’m busy getting in touch
                with what is real.
                I’m busy growing things and
                connecting with the natural world.
                I’m busy questioning my answers.

Drowning

                They come in waves,
                my feelings for you.
                And not pretty whitecaps
                dancing at my feet.
                But when I least expect it.
                When life seems to be
                a quiet stream of continuity.
                They come to disrupt.
                So forceful they pull me under,
                so that I am drowning and once again,
                can’t catch my breath.

                to remind you of how you bled
                and how you survived.
                You survived.
